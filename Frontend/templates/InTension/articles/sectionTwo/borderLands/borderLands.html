BorderLands

Seen affirmatively, borderlands are the spaces of betweenness, including, but not exclusively, spatial, cosmological, epistemological, cultural. Borderlands dissolve the binary divisions, like Global North and the South, the black and the white, unknowing and knowing. They create the conditions of possible exchange of ideas, knowledge, beliefs, cultures, language, images, histories. As such, borderlands are material and immaterial spaces of cultural and political creation/tension in which future potentialities can be conceived and made, if there is the will and the effort.
